Instructions for Entering Availability Slots

  • Each slot must have a valid start time and end time.
  • Slots must be in chronological order and must not overlap.
  • The start time must be before the end time.
  • The start time cannot be earlier than 08:00 and not later than 20:00.
  • The end time cannot be earlier than 09:00 and not later than 21:00.
  • Each slot must be at least 1 hour long and cannot exceed 2 hours.
  • Slots must be in order without gaps or overlaps between them.
